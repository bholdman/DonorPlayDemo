How it Works

1. Select a Team

Love Bulldogs Basketball? Can't get enough Bulldogs Softball and want to prove it to everyone? Show your support for your favorite participating Bulldogs team by selecting them from the list above.

2. Pledge a Stat

Select a stat and pledge money to the stat, For example, if you pledge $1 for every home run hit by the Bulldogs Baseball team, and 50 homeruns are hit this season , you'll donate $50.

3. Help Notre Dame

The money raised from the combined efforts of our student athletes and our donors directly impacts the quality and scope of services Notre Dame High School provides to its students and community.
