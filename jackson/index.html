How it Works

1. Select a Team

Love Indians Basketball? Can't get enough Indians Football and want to prove it to everyone? Show your support for your favorite participating Indians team by selecting them from the list above.

2. Pledge a Stat

Select a stat and pledge money to the stat, For example, if you pledge $1 for every goal scored by the Indians Soccer team, and 50 goals are scored this season , you'll donate $50.

3. Help Jackson High School

The money raised from the combined efforts of our student athletes and our donors directly impacts the quality and scope of servicesd Jackson High School provides to its students and community.
